Ways to pay

You can secure a booking for your London student accommodation through:

  1. Direct debit
  2. Credit or debit card
  3. UnionPay (for International students)

Paying for your student accommodation in London is easy and flexible. You have the option to pay in full or in one, three or seven instalments. Even better, all Unite Students properties come with bills included, meaning you won’t have to deal with the fuss of any unexpected additional costs.

While a deposit isn’t needed, you may be required to pay advance rent. This is a fraction of the full-year amount, and we put this towards your first rent instalment.

All renters are required to have a guarantor they can trust. This is someone over 25-years-old – such as a parent or guardian – who can ensure payments will be maintained if you fall behind on a rental payment.
